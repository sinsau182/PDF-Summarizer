 Marketing is the process of identifying and meeting human and social needs through the creation, delivery, and exchange of products and services. Marketing management is a business process used to manage marketing activities, and it involves analyzing, planning, implementing, and controlling programs designed to bring about desired exchanges with target audiences. The nature of marketing is customer-oriented, values-driven, relationship-focused, a specialized business function, and dynamic. The scope of marketing includes creating awareness, market research, product planning, branding, packaging, pricing, advertising and sales promotion, channels of distribution, financing, after-sales service, and collecting feedback. The importance of marketing lies in promoting awareness, boosting sales, building company reputation, fostering healthy competition, and driving innovation.
